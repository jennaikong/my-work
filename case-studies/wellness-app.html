The Challenge

Understanding the Problem

The client came to us with a clear vision: build a mindfulness and wellness app that stands out in a crowded market. However, existing wellness apps often suffer from high user churn and poor engagement after the first week.

We conducted interviews with 20+ potential users and found several key pain points: most felt overwhelmed by onboarding, didn't understand how to choose between features, and abandoned apps that didn't feel personalized to their needs.

The core insight: people don't want another app—they want a companion that understands them and meets them where they are in their wellness journey.

The Solution

A Personalized Wellness Experience

We redesigned the app around a personalized onboarding flow that sets the tone for the entire user experience. Instead of forcing users through a generic setup, we built adaptive flows based on their primary wellness goals.

Key design decisions included:

  • Smart onboarding that learns user preferences and recommends personalized content
  • Clear visual hierarchy that guides users to high-value features without cognitive overload
  • Progress tracking that feels rewarding and motivating rather than guilt-inducing
  • Community features that foster connection while respecting privacy
