The Challenge

Understanding the Problem

The client was experiencing a 38% cart abandonment rate—higher than industry average. While product and pricing were solid, users were dropping off during checkout. Our task was to diagnose why and redesign the experience to significantly reduce abandonment.

Through heatmaps, session recordings, and user interviews, we identified key friction points: unclear trust signals, unexpected costs revealed late in the flow, forced account creation, and multiple form validations.

The insight: users abandon not because they don't want to buy, but because they don't feel confident or comfortable completing the transaction.

The Solution

Transparent, Frictionless Checkout

We redesigned the checkout from the ground up with a focus on transparency and trust. The new flow reduces cognitive load, surfaces costs upfront, and removes unnecessary steps.

Key improvements:

  • Single-page checkout with clear progress (instead of 5-step flow)
  • Show all costs (tax, shipping) before final confirmation
  • Optional account creation (guest checkout as primary)
  • Trust badges and security seals prominently displayed
  • Real-time validation feedback instead of form submission errors
  • Mobile-optimized single-column layout
