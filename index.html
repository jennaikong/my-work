About Me

A designer who
listens first.

I'm a UI/UX designer passionate about creating digital products that feel effortless to use. My process is rooted in empathy — understanding people before pixels — and translating insights into designs that are both beautiful and functional.

With a background spanning mobile apps, web platforms, and design systems, I bring a thoughtful, research-led approach to every project. I believe the best design is the kind users never have to think about.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring local coffee shops, sketching in notebooks, or obsessing over typography.
